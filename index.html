~ Hawaiian Strategy Game ~

How to Play Kōnane

🎯 Objective: Jump over and remove your opponent’s pieces. The last player able to move wins.

🛠️ Setup: Fill the board in a checkerboard pattern. Black removes a piece from a corner or center. White removes one adjacent to that empty space.

🎮 Gameplay: Players take turns jumping horizontally or vertically (not diagonally) over adjacent opponent pieces into empty spaces. Each jump removes the jumped piece.

⛔ End: When a player has no legal move, they lose the game.

💡 Note: Based on the traditional Hawaiian papamu game board.
